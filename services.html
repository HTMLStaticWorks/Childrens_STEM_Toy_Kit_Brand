Educational Services

School & Educator Programs

We partner with schools to provide bulk kits, curriculum alignment, and teacher training. Make science class the highlight of their week.

Our school packages are fully customizable, mapped to NGSS (Next Generation Science Standards), and designed to support teachers with detailed lesson plans and worksheets.

  • NGSS Aligned: Ready-to-teach curriculum mapping.
  • Discounted Bulk Rates: Fits school and district budgets.
  • Teacher Training: Webinars and instructional guides included.
  • LMS Dashboard: Free digital progress tracking portal.

Monthly STEM Boxes

Keep the learning going year-round with our monthly subscription box. Every month, a new themed kit arrives at your door.

Delivered direct to your home, each box contains a new adventure focusing on coding, physics, electronics, or chemistry.

  • 🚀 New Subject Monthly: Never get bored with rotating STEM themes.
  • ⚙️ Age-Graded Kits: Tailored perfectly to your child's age group.
  • 🔒 Cancel Anytime: Swap age groups or pause subscriptions with 1-click.
