Our Mission

To inspire every child to become a creator, innovator, and problem solver.

How StemGen Started

Founded by a group of engineers and teachers in 2020, StemGen was born out of frustration with screen time and a lack of engaging hands-on educational tools.

We set out to create kits that don't just teach the 'what' but the 'how' and 'why', empowering kids to understand the technology around them.

Our Journey So Far
  • 2020: The Garage Prototype

    Built our very first robotics prototypes in a home workshop.

  • 2021: Classroom Validation

    Partnered with local schools to test and co-develop the curriculum.

  • Present: Inspiring Worldwide

    Now trusted by parents and teachers in over 15 countries.

Our Educational Philosophy

1. Play is Learning

When kids are having fun, they retain 80% more information.

2. Failure is Data

We encourage kids to make mistakes; it's the core of the scientific method.

3. No Boundaries

Our kits cross the boundaries of science, art, and math (STEAM).
